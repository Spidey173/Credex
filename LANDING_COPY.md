## 3. The B2B Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Q1: How does the AI Spend Audit work?
**A:** It is a 100% free, browser-based spend diagnostic tool. You input your team size, active subscriptions (e.g., Cursor, Copilot, Claude, ChatGPT, API direct spend), and active usage metrics. Our engine runs a set of deterministic B2B financial rules to find seat leakage, overlapping IDE tools, team plan minimum overdraws, and enterprise contract inflation, returning a structured savings report instantly.

### Q2: Do I need to connect our database or corporate credit cards?
**A:** Absolutely not. We do not require any API integrations, card attachments, or read-write database tokens. The audit runs entirely based on the high-fidelity numbers you input, preserving 100% data privacy. You can test stack configurations safely without exposing credentials.

### Q3: Why is this tool completely free? Where is the catch?
**A:** There is no catch. The tool is sponsored by Credex. We broker discounted AI infrastructure credits sourced from companies that overforecasted or pivoted. By highlighting your subscription waste, we show you where to cut costs—and if your savings potential is high, we offer you the chance to buy your remaining stack through Credex credits at up to a 30% discount. It is a lead-generation asset for us.

### Q4: How do you guarantee the pricing rules are current?
**A:** Our TS engine is updated weekly against official pricing indexes. We cite the exact, official vendor URLs for Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Anthropic Claude, OpenAI, Gemini, and Windsurf directly in your results dashboard. Every recommendation includes the date verified.

### Q5: What is the "Credex Consultation" booked for high-savings audits?
**A:** If our audit engine detects that your team is spending more than $500/month in optimization waste, we surface a calendar booking link. Credex specialists will manually review your spending ledger, map out credit substitutions, and swap your retail licenses for wholesale discounted credits, instantly slashing your infrastructure cost.
